# Privacy Policy

**App is 100% offline: no accounts, no cloud processing.**

## Data Processing
All data processing happens on-device. Your media files never leave your phone.

## Local Data Storage
We store only:
- App settings (theme, compression preferences)
- Local Pro entitlement (device-local license)
- Optional logs (only in debug builds; Release builds do not log to storage)

## What is NOT collected
- No analytics
- No ads tracking
- No user behavior tracking

## Wallet Interactions
- Wallet connection occurs via your own wallet app (Phantom/Seeker).
- We do **not** receive, store, or touch your private keys.
- On-chain checks are read-only to verify payment status.
